Property details

Tuck Mill Cottage is located centrally between Newcastle, Castlewellan, Murlough Nature Reserve and Tollymore Forest Park and is an ideal place from which to base your holiday.

This 200 years old cottage is set in a tranquil setting, a five-minute drive from both Newcastle and Castlewellan. Accessed by a private drive approximately 400 m off the main road and 600 m from the village of Maghera, the cottage features superb views of the Mournes.

We have sought to equip the cottage to a high standard and to anticipate your needs by providing all the things you might want for a luxurious stay: a super-king sized bed, log burning stove, TV with free view channels, dishwasher, washing machine, hair dryer, bedding and towels are provided as standard.

As the cottage is over 200 years old some internal doors are not regular size: one is just under 1750 mm in height, and two others are 675 mm wide, thereby making them unsuitable for wheelchair access.

Members of our family have farmed the land around Tuck Mill Cottage for generations, and we have lived onsite for the past 20-odd years. We love the peace and tranquillity of the area and yet the ease of access to local amenities. We have always enjoyed sharing our home and surroundings with friends and family, and now that we have renovated Tuck Mill Cottage we are keen to expand this to include you. As keen out-door people we enjoy our local forest parks, Tollymore and Castlewellan, and the beach at Murlough as well as hill walking in the Mournes. We would be very happy to advise you on local and mountain walking routes.

Tuck Mill Cottage is situated in the stunning Mourne Mountains Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty.
